MEET YOUR BARBERS

CHUCK FALK

Chuck became a barber to do more than just give a good hair cut. He wanted to shed light on a very important style detail that most men were missing. The feeling of looking their best. 

Anyone can give a haircut, but with good communication between the patron and the barber everyone will be happy. Chuck will do his best to educate his clients about how to wear their hair and what products to use.

RYAN HARDWICK 

Eddy's barbershop resident funny guy, professional multi cultural barber, punk rock musician, tattoo collector, radical dad to a wild four year old, skilled at traditional American haircuts. Enjoy the luxuries of perfect hair forever with a cut by Mr. Hardwick.

BOBBY BUSHAY

Master barber, hip hop enthusiast, sneaker head, diverse in all hair types and textures. Cleveland native, born and raised. Saving and changing lives, one haircut at a time.

PATRICK CORRIGAN

Patrick is the newest member of the shop. He has been cutting hair for almost 2 years now. He prefers classic traditional barbering influenced by 50's old school such as High Pompadours, Side Part variations, Princetons and Flat-tops. 

Patrick mostly enjoys talking about vintage guitars, The Rolling Stones or The Eagles. And when he is not in the shop he can be found pickin' the guitar for his local band Thirteen Cadillacs or out on the road with Chicago rockers The Wabash Cannonballs.

EDDY'S

Eddy's Barbershop is a classic traditional barber shop, with locations on the 
east side of Cleveland in the Coventry neighborhood and a soon-to-be
announced location on the west side.

The team of barbers, led by Chuck Falk, are experts in their field with 
years of experience. Men can stop in for a quick cut or relax and enjoy 
with a 45-minute old-fashioned shave.
