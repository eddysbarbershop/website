Years of research by our experts proved that all skin is not created equal. Our approach is science plus nature, built from the ground up in California. We may be old school in concept, but we’re high-tech in practice. Bottom line: Finley’s dry skin began a revolution. Baxter’s legacy meets advanced research: this is the evolution of men’s grooming.
